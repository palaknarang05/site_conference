Abstract Submission Guidelines

Abstract Submission Guidelines
  • Abstracts in English are accepted.
  • An abstract of 150-200 words is required, with the introduction of the topic/text/writer at the beginning.
  • Abstracts should be submitted as MS-Word Documents, the font should be Times New Roman (12 size), single spaced.
  • Please add your brief bio note (3 to 4 lines) at the bottom of the abstract.
  • Accepted abstracts will be given 10 minutes for presentation and 5 minutes for Q&A.
